SurveyFill

Annoyed at the surveys online, I realized that there has to be a way to protect your identity online when filling surveys. This is how SurveyFill came to be.

SurveyFill is an innovative application which allows you to generate fake information to take the place of your own identity. SurveyFill's information is all random and fake, so it is also completely legal.

Survey fill can generate fake names, birthdays, jobs, e-mails, accounts, and also fake credit card numbers.

ASCVD Risk Prevention Suite

At a high ranking status, this app helps doctors (specifically cardiologists) make the right decisions for their patients using the new guidelines outlined by the American Heart Association. 

This app has gotten over 18,000 downloads, and has over 100 reviews averaging five stars. 

Specifically, this app helps doctors and patients determine ASCVD (atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease) risk in a ten year period. The calculator also generates a consult recommendation based on the patient's variables (HDL Cholesterol, age, sex, total cholesterol, etc.)
